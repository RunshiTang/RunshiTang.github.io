About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Statistics at UW-Madison, under the supervision of Dr. Anru Zhang (from Duke University) and Dr. Richard Chappell. I earned my Master's degree from UW-Madison in 2021 and my Bachelor's degree from Shandong University, China in 2020.
